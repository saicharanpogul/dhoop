# Dhoop - Onchain Solar Yield Vault

Tokenised rooftop solar revenue on Solana. DePINFi pools + performance-linked cohort tokens bridging India's $360B solar financing gap.

**Website:** [dhoop.finance](https://dhoop.finance)

## What is Dhoop?

Dhoop connects global crypto capital with Indian rooftop solar installations. Investors deposit USDC into DePINFi pools on Solana, funding real solar panel installations on Indian rooftops. Smart inverters track generation, revenue flows back as yield, and cohort tokens provide secondary market exposure with performance-linked buybacks.

- **For Investors:** 12-20% APR in USDC backed by real solar revenue
- **For Hosts:** Free solar installation, 35-50% lower electricity bills from day one
- **For the Protocol:** Origination fees, AUM fees, trading fees, performance fees
